# Food Delivery Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes food delivery data to evaluate operational performance, delivery efficiency, and customer trends. The goal is to identify key factors affecting delivery time, cancellations, and overall business performance.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Food delivery platforms need to ensure fast delivery and low cancellation rates. This project aims to analyze delivery operations and identify inefficiencies to improve customer experience and business performance.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information
The dataset includes:

- Order ID  
- Order Time  
- Delivery Time  
- Restaurant Name  
- City  
- Order Status (Delivered / Cancelled)  
- Revenue  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning
- Handled missing values  
- Converted time columns into proper format  
- Removed duplicate records  
- Ensured consistency in categorical data  

---

## 📊 SQL Analysis

### 🚚 Delivery Time Analysis
- Calculated average delivery time  
- Identified delays and trends  

### ❌ Cancellation Rate
- Calculated percentage of cancelled orders  
- Analyzed impact on business  

### 🍽 Top Restaurants
- Identified restaurants with highest orders and revenue  

### ⏰ Peak Order Hours
- Analyzed order frequency by hour  
- Identified high-demand time slots  

### 🌆 City-wise Revenue
- Compared revenue across different cities  
- Identified top-performing locations  

---

## 📈 Visualization

- Heatmap of peak order hours  
- Delivery time trend analysis  
- Restaurant performance dashboard  

---

## 🧠 SQL Concepts Used
- Aggregations (AVG, COUNT, SUM)  
- GROUP BY  
- ORDER BY  
- CASE WHEN  
- Joins (if multiple tables used)  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Peak hours significantly impact delivery time  
- High cancellation rates indicate operational inefficiencies  
- A small number of restaurants generate most revenue  
- Certain cities perform better than others  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Optimize delivery resources during peak hours  
- Reduce cancellation by improving delivery time  
- Partner more with high-performing restaurants  
- Focus on top-performing cities for expansion
